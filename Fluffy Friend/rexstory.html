Rex' Story

Rex

Rex had been trained to hunt, his sharp eyes and strong build making him a perfect companion for a hunter. But when he failed to live up to expectations during a hunting trip, his owner abandoned him in the forest.

For days, Rex wandered, unsure of where to go, until he stumbled upon the old house. He found the pack there and took it upon himself to guard them, determined never to fail his family again.
