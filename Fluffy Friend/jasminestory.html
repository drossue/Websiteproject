Jasmine's Story

Jasmine

Jasmine had once lived in a cozy home with a loving elderly woman who would pet her soft white fur for hours. But when her owner grew too sick and had to move into assisted care, Jasmine was taken to a shelter.

She spent weeks waiting for someone to take her home, but no one came. One day, Jasmine saw an opportunity and slipped out of the shelter, eventually finding her way to the old house on the edge of the forest. She made herself at home, always ready to offer comfort to the others.
