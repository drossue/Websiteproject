Luna's Story

Luna

Luna had always been timid. She was adopted by a family who soon grew frustrated with her shyness and reluctance to play like other dogs. They left her by the side of the road one day, and Luna found herself alone, terrified and confused.

She eventually found her way to the abandoned house, where she met Jasmine. With Jasmine's gentle support, Luna slowly began to feel safe again, always staying close to her friend.
