Michael's Story

Michael

Michael had never known a family. Born on the streets, he learned to survive the hard way, scrounging for food and hiding from threats. His small size made it easier to dodge dangers, but it also made him vulnerable.

Despite his hardships, Michael remained playful and curious, always trying to make the best of what he had. When he found the abandoned house, he knew he had found a safe place to rest, and soon became the clown of the group, always finding ways to make the others smile.
