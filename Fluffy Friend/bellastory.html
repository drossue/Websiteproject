Bella's Story

Bella

Bella was the youngest, just a puppy when her family moved away and left her behind. She didn't understand why they had gone or why she was alone, but her natural curiosity drove her to explore rather than dwell on her sadness.

She wandered until she found the old house and the pack of other dogs. Despite her age, Bella's adventurous spirit often led the group to new places and hidden treasures, her innocent enthusiasm helping to lift everyone's spirits.
