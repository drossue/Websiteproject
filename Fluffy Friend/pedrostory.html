Pedro's Story

Pedro

Pedro had always been a brave and loyal dog who came to the shelter in early 2019. He was found wandering the streets, hungry and in need of care. With a little bit of love, Pedro quickly became one of the friendliest dogs anyone had ever met. He loved playing with other dogs and was always eager to meet new people.

Despite his rough start, Pedro eventually found an abandoned house to call home, and he made it his mission to protect this new place, just as he had once protected his family.
